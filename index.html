Kaitlyn's Portfolio

This portfolio is a collection of different projects I’ve worked on while learning and experimenting with design and coding. It includes a mix of websites, creative coding projects, and other digital work that reflect the kinds of ideas and styles I’ve been interested in exploring. Some projects focus more on visual design and atmosphere, while others are about building technical skills and trying out new tools or approaches. One of the projects featured here is a glitch-inspired HTML website based on creepy liminal spaces, where I experimented with abnormal visuals and layouts to create a story through simple web design. Working on projects like this pushed me to think about how websites can do more than just present information. They can also create a mood or tell a story. These projects show my process of learning, experimenting, and gradually building my skills in both design and development while trying out different creative ideas along the way.
